Tortang Talong Recipe

Ingredients

  • 2-3 medium eggplants (talong)
  • 2 eggs
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1-2 tablespoons cooking oil

Steps

  1. Grill or roast the eggplants until the skin becomes charred and the flesh is soft.
  2. Let them cool slightly, then peel off the charred skin.
  3. Flatten the peeled eggplants using a fork.
  4. Beat the eggs in a bowl and season with salt and pepper.
  5. Dip the flattened eggplants into the egg mixture.
  6. Heat oil in a pan and fry each eggplant until golden brown on both sides.
